Honestly the process for this cycle was rather simple. I looked up two tutorials on Youtube on how to get multiple lights to blink and one on how to make a button work. I then adjusted their designs to make them work for my project. I then used the code system built in to Tinker CAD.

The final result was three lights that lit up independently and one that could be deactivated with a button.
