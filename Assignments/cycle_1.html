The first step I did was to make a new layer on top of my rasterized image. Using the rectangle tool I then created 8 equal sized squares all on different layers so I would be able to turn them off separately as I needed.

Then I started to create the interlocking parts of the puzzle with the pen tool using the handels I created the curves I wanted. I made sure to delete any overlapping lines to avoid the cutter trying to cut the material twice..

Some of the things I used to help me to get my finished product is to have all of my squares on separate layers so I could focus on two individual contact points. That also helped in the process of making sure there were no overlapping lines.

I also used the anchor tool to place new points in the middle of the already placed lines so I could just cut out the unnecessary line segments instead of replacing them and I used the join tool to attach the interlocking pisces.
